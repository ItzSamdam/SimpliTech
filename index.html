About

SimpliTech Solution brings generations together through Technology
There are three categories of people in society today:

Those Born into Technology
"Digital Natives"

Among the categories of Technology Adopters are those who have been born into The Tech World. They don't have issues manipulating and navigating Technology.

Those that Adopted Technology
"Adopters"

The second category are the "Adopters". Unlike the natives they learn technology and use it in the daily lives

Those that haven't adopted
"Non-Adopters"

This category is the group who are not ready to accept technology and they live their lives without leveraging any aspects of it.

Becoming More confident and capable of using Technology will keep seniors from relying,
so much on their family and missing out on communicating
in current ways with Grandchildren and other friends and family.
